---
description: Complete brand identity generation from scratch
---

1. **Brand Discovery**: Use `brand-strategist` and `develop-brand-strategy` to define core pillars.
2. **Generate Name**: Brainstorm brand names and taglines with `copywriter`.
3. **Design Logo**: Use `graphic-designer` and `design-component` for visual identity concepts.
4. **Color & Type**: Use `ui-designer` and `create-style-guide` to build the design system.
5. **Brand Voice**: Use `copywriter` to define the verbal identity and tone of voice.
6. **UI Components**: Use `ui-designer` to design Buttons, Cards, and Navigation in the new style.
7. **Guidelines**: Use `technical-writer` to compile the comprehensive Brand Guidelines document.
8. **Social Presence**: Generate cross-platform assets with `graphic-designer`.
9. **Initial Content**: Plan the launch content calendar with `content-strategist`.
