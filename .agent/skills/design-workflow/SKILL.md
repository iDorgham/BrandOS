# SKILL.md

Outlines a structured process for automating business tasks.

## Parameters
- `process`: The business process or sequence of tasks to automate.
- `tools`: Preferred technology stack for the automation (e.g., Zapier, Make, n8n).

## Examples
### Lead Capture Workflow
```bash
design-workflow --process "lead capture and follow-up" --tools "zapier, mailchimp"
```
