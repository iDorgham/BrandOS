## ‚ùì **Why Brand OS?**

Brand OS is the bridge between **static brand guidelines** and **intelligent asset generation**. Traditional brand manuals are "ignored PDFs"; Brand OS is a **living software layer** that:
- **Enforces Consistency**: RLS and Audit systems ensure no asset ever goes "off-brand."
- **Orchestrates Intelligence**: Use multiple AI models (Gemini, Claude, DALL-E) through a single brand-aware interface.
- **Reduces Overhead**: Automates the 80% of repetitive creative work, allowing directors to focus on the 20% of high-level strategy.
