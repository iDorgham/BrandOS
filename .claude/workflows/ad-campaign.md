---
description: Cross-platform ad campaign creation and deployment
---

1. **Develop Campaign Strategy**: Use `marketing-strategist` and `plan-campaign` to define goals and budget.
2. **Research Target Audience**: Identify demographics and platform selection.
3. **Research Keywords**: Use `keyword-research` to find high-performing terms.
4. **Create Ad Copy**: Use `copywriter` and `create-ad` to generate search, social, and display variations.
5. **Design Ad Visuals**: Use `graphic-designer` and `generate-image-prompt` to create asset concepts.
6. **Create Landing Pages**: Use `fullstack-developer` and `create-component` to build target pages.
7. **Setup Tracking**: Configure analytics and conversion events via `devops-engineer`.
8. **Create Variations**: Generate platform-specific ads for Google, Facebook, and LinkedIn.
9. **Media Buying Plan**: Finalize budget allocation and strategy.
